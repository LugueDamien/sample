Noli Me Tangere Characters
Crisostomo Ibarra - Ibarra studied in Europe for at least seven years he was an earnest person. He wanted to improve his country and believed in the power of education.
Padre Damaso - A spanish priest that was narcissistic and acted superior amongst the others. You could say he was also brash and arrogant.
Maria Clara - She is the fiance of Crisostomo Ibarra. She was a kindhearted person and was well loved by the people around her.
Donya Victorina - A woman with colonial mentality. She had a domineering attitude and wanted to gain self-esteem, though she acts like she is spanish she is not.
Elias - He was a selfless and wealthy person.
